Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What medical equipment do you supply?

We supply diagnostic imaging, surgical instruments, patient monitoring, laboratory equipment, physiotherapy units, and medical consumables — sourced from internationally certified manufacturers.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes. Our qualified engineers handle complete installation, configuration, calibration, and commissioning, along with operator training and full handover documentation.

What warranty do products come with?

Warranty periods range from 1–3 years depending on product and manufacturer. We manage all warranty claims directly with manufacturers on your behalf.

Can you source products not listed?

Absolutely. Our global supplier network means we can source almost any medical or industrial product. Send your requirements and we'll quote within a few business days.

How long does delivery take?

Local stock items deliver within days. Imported products typically take 4–8 weeks depending on origin country and customs processing.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts?

Yes. We offer tailored AMC packages covering preventive maintenance, emergency call-outs, and spare parts at preferential rates to ensure maximum equipment uptime.
